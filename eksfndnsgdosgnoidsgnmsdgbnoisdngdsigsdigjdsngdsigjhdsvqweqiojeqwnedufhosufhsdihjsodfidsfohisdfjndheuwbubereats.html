Submit it and whalla! In 1-2 hours, you'll get an email saying they are refunding your food!

Soggy Food Trick

You are telling them that the burger got soggy because of the drink. (Or any food for that matter.)

Remember, use your own words and you can add more detail if you want! And whalla! In 1-2 hours, you'll get an email saying they are refunding your food!
